Lasagna

Description

A super good lasagna. It is authentic, everbody will love this recipe. It comes from Italy and we are very excited about it just wow.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Make sauce
  3. Prep the pasta sheets
  4. In a seperate pan, layer pasta sheet, then ground beef, then sauce, then cheese. Continue until finished
  5. Bake until complete
